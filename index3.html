About Us

Started by father and daughter, business partners, and best friends, Fantasia Butterfly Company metamorphosizsed from what was just an idea. We operate out of the coastal peninusula of Cape Cod, observing the sun dipping below the horizon in our free time. We specialize in the art of spreading and preserving visually captivating specimens, transforming mother nature's beauty into timeless displays and bringing them into your living space.
